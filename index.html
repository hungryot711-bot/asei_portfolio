Artist Statement & Profile

痛みを知るからこそ、誰かの心に寄り添える。

Art is born from the fire of survival.

1973年兵庫県生まれ、姫路市在住。玄燿書道会所属. Born in Hyogo Prefecture in 1973, currently residing in Himeji City. Member of the Genyo Calligraphy Society.

2000年に師範を取得するも、最愛の師（山根溪石先生）の他界、自身や家族の体調不良が重なり、一度は書道を完全に封印しました. しかし昨年、断捨離をしていた際にどうしても書道の道具だけは捨てられない自分に気づき、同じ会派の師の直弟子にあたる先生の元へ再入門. 25年ぶりに再び書の道を歩み始めました. After earning my Master's License in 2000, a deep void swallowed my world due to the sudden passing of my master, Master Keiseki Yamane, alongside severe personal and family illnesses, leading me to completely seal away my calligraphy. However, last year, while decluttering, I realized I couldn't bring myself to discard my calligraphy tools. Guided back to the brush, I re-entered the same school under my master's direct disciple, restarting my artistic journey after 25 years.

私はかつて、激しい痛みを伴う「線維筋痛症」を患いました（現在は寛解）. 自分自身が身体の不自由さや痛みを経験したからこそ、今、介護福祉士として接する高齢者の方々や、障害を持たれている方々の気持ちが痛いほど理解できるようになりました. Life tested me through severe illness (Fibromyalgia, currently in remission). Having experienced physical limitations and pain firsthand, I can now deeply empathize with the feelings of the elderly and individuals with disabilities whom I care for.

高齢者施設での夜勤専従職員として人の生死に向き合う日々の中で、新しく「アート書」への挑戦を開始. 「高齢者や障害者、社会に関わるすべての人に笑顔になってもらいたい」という祈りを込め、一滴の墨に生命を吹き込み続けています. Working as a certified caregiver holding the hands of the elderly at the end of their life journeys, I began exploring contemporary art calligraphy. I do not write simply to show beautiful letters; I strike the paper to bring life-energy to the weary, to bring smiles to the broken, and to honor the resilience of human souls.

Featured Works & Awards

NY International Calligraphy Exhibition - Runner-up Prize

春動花開 桜と蛙

Spring Awakens and Flowers Blossom (2026)

介護士として働きながら、日々古典の勉強と作品活動に励む中で生まれたアート書. 桜の舞う優美さと蛙の跳び出すような春の生命力を、豊かなデザインと物語性で表現. ニューヨーク国際書道展にて準優勝賞受賞.

A contemporary piece created while working diligently as a caregiver. It beautifully captures the elegance of dancing cherry blossoms and the vibrant life-force of a leaping frog, weaving a narrative through design. Awarded the Runner-up Prize at the New York International Calligraphy Exhibition.

Genyo Exhibition - Kobe Cultural Support Foundation Prize

長恨歌

240cm × 60cm | Song of Everlasting Regret (2026)

政治の混乱による永遠の別れを描いた白居易の詩. 現代の混迷や、突然訪れる大切な人との別れを重ね合わせ、深い愛情を生命力溢れる筆致で表現. 第76回 玄耀書道展 会員の部受賞.

Based on the classic poem depicting profound love and eternal parting amidst political turmoil. Overlapping with the chaos and sudden losses of our modern world, this piece expresses deep affection and resilience. Winner of the Kobe Cultural Support Foundation Prize.

表具 / Mounting：玉木楽山堂

Hyogo Prefectural Exhibition - Accepted

春園

240cm × 60cm | Shunen - Spring Garden (2026)

穏やかな隠遁生活を楽しむ老人の日常を描いた詩. 介護士として接する高齢者たちの「時代を生き抜いた力強さ」と「老いた儚さ」の双方を重ね合わせた、25年ぶりの復帰第一作. 兵庫県展公募入選.

This piece illustrates the peaceful, quiet retirement of an old man. As a caregiver, I infused this calligraphy with both the incredible strength of the elderly who survived history and their gentle, fragile side. Deeply honored to be accepted into the Hyogo Prefectural Exhibition as my first piece in 25 years.

表具 / Mounting：玉木楽山堂

書の歩み

Calligraphy Chronology

1995

山根溪石先生に入門。書の神髄を学び始める.
Began studying under Master Keiseki Yamane.

1997 - 1999

読売書法展にて通算3回入選.
Accepted into the Yomiuri Shofoten Exhibition three times.

1999

玄耀書道会 会員 / 兵庫県書作家協会 準会員に推挙.
Recommended as a Member of Genyo Calligraphy Society and Associate Member of the Hyogo Calligrapher Association.

2000

日本書芸院 二科会員推薦賞 受賞. 同年、師範試験取得.
Received the Nihon Shogeiin Nika Member Recommendation Award. Earned Master's License.

2000 - 2024

最愛の師の他界、自身の病気などにより、一時書道を封印する.
Temporarily sealed away calligraphy due to the master's passing and personal illness.

2025

道具の断捨離を機に玄燿書道会へ再入門. ニューヨーク国際書道展にて「翔」が入選し奇跡の再起を果たす.
Re-entered Genyo Society. Achieved a miraculous comeback with my piece "Sho" (To Soar) accepted at the NY International Calligraphy Exhibition.

2026

ニューヨーク国際書道展にて『春動花開』が準優勝賞を受賞. 第76回 玄耀書道展にて神戸市民文化振興財団賞を受賞. 兵庫県展入選.
Awarded the **Runner-up Prize** at the NY Exhibition. Won the **Kobe Cultural Support Foundation Prize** at the 76th Genyo Exhibition. Accepted into the Hyogo Prefectural Exhibition.

Future Vision

バリアフリーアート書の構想

Accessible & Barrier-Free Art

身体が不自由な方でも、見るだけで内側から湧き上がるエネルギーを感じ、心と魂で通じ合えるバリアフリーな作品・空間の制作を目指しています。

Aiming to design barrier-free art installations where individuals with disabilities can experience raw life-energy and connect on a spiritual level.

福祉施設レクリエーション提案

Care Facility Workshops

高齢者施設や障害者施設において、書の楽しさに触れ、誰もが内側から自然と笑顔になれるような、新しい書道レクリエーションの企画・提案を行います。

Proposing and implementing innovative calligraphy workshops in elderly and disability care facilities to bring pure joy and therapeutic smiles.
